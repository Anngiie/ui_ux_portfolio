Overview

Operational Control for City Incidents

CloudCity is designed for response teams, not end users. It centralizes incident intake from the public app and gives admins a clear, auditable workflow to resolve issues quickly.

Challenge

Design a dashboard that supports three user roles with different permissions, while keeping incident triage, assignment, and resolution fast and consistent.

Goals

  • Real-time visibility into incoming incidents
  • Clear status lifecycle (open, urgent, in progress, pending, resolved)
  • Quick assignment and escalation workflows
  • Audit-friendly logs and activity history

Accessibility & Responsiveness

Accessibility considerations

  • High-contrast status badges and readable table typography
  • Consistent layout patterns for fast scanning
  • Structured headings for clear navigation in dense views

Responsiveness

  • Responsive tables with stacked details on smaller screens
  • Flexible grids and media that scale down without breaking layout
