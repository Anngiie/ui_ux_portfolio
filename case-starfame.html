StarFame — Events & Reservations

A mobile app for booking events and restaurant reservations with flows for guests, registered users, and businesses.

  • Timeline 1.5 months

  • Tools Figma, Photoshop, InDesign
Overview

One App for Events & Dining

The experience needed to support multi-role flows without sacrificing clarity for end users.

Challenge

Balance complex booking logic with a simple, friendly UI for guests and business owners.

Goals

  • Quick ticket booking and reservations
  • Clear separation of user roles
  • Consistent navigation across flows
Artifacts

Flowchart

StarFame supports three roles: Guest, User, and Business. Guests can explore events and places and view details, but need an account to bookmark or buy tickets. Logged-in users can save favorites, purchase tickets, and access QR-code tickets for entry, along with notifications and profile settings. Business users run the operational side—creating venues/events, configuring ticketing and capacity, setting working hours, and managing attendees (optionally via QR check-in).
