Currently Learning / Influences

What I Am Reading

A small shelf of UX and UI books shaping how I think about structure, clarity, and visual polish.

101 Principles of Great UX Design

Will Grant

A practical handbook of UX rules for web and digital products, covering readability, navigation, controls, consistency, iconography, and accessibility.

Refactoring UI (PDF)

Adam Wathan & Steve Schoger

Easy and fast read. A highly practical guide to clean, modern interfaces with clear hierarchy, spacing, typography, color, and layout rules.

100 Things Every Designer Needs to Know About People

Susan Weinschenk

UX and human psychology insights on perception, memory, decision-making, and mistakes to design clearer experiences.

The Principles of Beautiful Web Design

Jason Beaird & Alex Walker

Foundations of visual design: composition, color, typography, and the polish that makes websites feel balanced.
