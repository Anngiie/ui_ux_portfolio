Overview

Seamless Guest Experience

The goal was to reduce friction for guests and provide instant access to key hotel services.

Challenge

Design a simple mobile flow that covers access, service requests, and information without overwhelming users.

Goals

  • Fast, reliable room access
  • Clear navigation for hotel services
  • Optimized for one-handed use
Process

Flow-First Design

Guest Scenarios

Define

User Flows

Prioritize primary actions and group secondary actions by context.

Design

Mobile UI

Create clean, tap-friendly layouts with clear status indicators.

Information Architecture

Flowchart

This experience is designed to reduce front-desk dependence by turning the app into a full stay companion: guests can confidently book the right space, access it instantly, and handle most needs without waiting or calling. The journey balances transactional tasks (browse, reserve, identity, payments, key access) with in-stay support (orders, messaging, notifications) and discovery (nearby activities + venue info). The digital key is treated as the trust anchor, supported by clear activation steps and connection feedback so users always understand what’s happening at the door.
