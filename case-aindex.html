Overview

Overview

AIndex is a short, UI-first concept for browsing AI research content. This was a 1-week freelance project focused on readable hierarchy, fast filtering, and a consistent experience across light and dark themes.

Interaction

The UI was designed with clear interaction patterns such as filtering, topic-driven discovery, and quick actions that keep reading and exploring frictionless.

LEARNINGS

What I Learned / Next Steps

What I Learned

  • Clear hierarchy is essential for long-form content and fast scanning
  • A strong filter model can dramatically reduce time-to-find
  • Consistent UI patterns across light and dark modes improve usability

Next Steps

  • Validate the filter taxonomy with quick usability checks
  • Add empty/loading/error states for key content flows
